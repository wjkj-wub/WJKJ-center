E-sports Entertainment Town

Based on the successful operation in Hangzhou, officers of the Ministry of Culture and Tourism, Hanghzou, Zhenzhou,Shenzhen, Nanjing governments successively visited the town.

The project covers an area of 72 acres and has a total construction area of 150,000 square meters. Five buildings is built, with air corridor connections in between, along with nearly 1,000 square sinking squares and large commercial projects.The enclosed space of the area, as well as the introduction of large-scale green plants and ecosystems, achieve the integration of ecology and nature.

ShangHai Expo E-sports Platform

The E-sports Industry Incubation Center of Wangjing, and the Mercedes-Benz Cultural Center jointly created the E-sports Industry and Culture Center. At the same time, the CMEL Masters Series Shanghai Station and the ESTAR E-sports Club reached strategic cooperation with the Center.

The Center will build an e-sports industry complex, integrating clubs and youth training bases, e-sports and pan-entertainment venues, e-sports booths, education incubators. The company will continue to introduce e-sports industry chain related enterprises, e-sports and pan-entertainment brand events in the future to create e-sports industry contents.

It is divided into three parts: mobile APP, WEB background and PC client, used for players and events organizers to solve the needs in different periods such as pre-game registration, in-game process and post-game statistics.

In the future, under the guidance of General Administration of Sport, the platform will become an e-sports competition ecological circle with Chinese E-sports Players Points system, forming a series of events, contestants, guess, live streaming, communities, and data, which will become an essential tool of the e-sports events.
